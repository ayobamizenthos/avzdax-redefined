01 // PRIVACY POLICY

At AVZDAX Technologies, we treat data privacy as a matter of national security. Unlike traditional surveillance vendors, we do not sell, trade, or monetize client footage.

Your data remains your property. Whether stored on-premise via TRION Edge Hardware or in our secure cloud via TELETRAAN, AVZDAX acts solely as the processor, never the controller.

02 // DATA SOVEREIGNTY

We respect the borders of jurisdiction. Data generated in Nigeria stays in Nigeria. Data generated in the UK stays in the UK.

We utilize region-locked AWS and Azure GovCloud instances to ensure compliance with NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation) and GDPR standards. No data packet leaves its origin country without explicit client authorization.
